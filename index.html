Monster Survivors

Survive endless waves of monsters in this action-packed roguelike adventure!

About the Game

Monster Survivors is an intense survival action game where you control a hero battling against endless waves of monsters. Choose your character wisely, collect power-ups, and upgrade your abilities to survive as long as possible in this challenging roguelike experience.

Inspired by classic arcade games and modern roguelikes, Monster Survivors offers a perfect blend of simple controls and deep strategic gameplay. Can you become the ultimate survivor?

Game Features

Multiple Characters

Choose from a variety of unique characters, each with their own special abilities and starting weapons.

Weapon Evolution

Combine weapons and items to create powerful evolved versions with enhanced capabilities.

Level Up System

Gain experience points by defeating enemies and level up to select new abilities or upgrade existing ones.

Endless Gameplay

The game gets progressively more challenging with stronger enemies and new boss encounters.

How to Play

  • Use WASD or arrow keys to move your character
  • Your character attacks automatically against nearby enemies
  • Collect gems to gain experience and level up
  • Choose new weapons or upgrade existing ones when leveling up
  • Survive as long as possible against increasingly difficult waves of monsters
  • Collect chests to obtain special items and power-ups
